## How Costa Brava boat rental prices are structured

Before the tables, three variables drive the final price:

- **Season**: LOW (April-June and September-October), MID (July) and HIGH (August). The gap between LOW and HIGH can be 30-35%.

On top of that, two things are worth knowing before you sign:

We also apply a **15% weekend surcharge** on Saturdays and Sundays, which is standard practice in Costa Brava boat rental. In August the minimum booking goes up to 2 hours; the rest of the year you can book from 1 hour.

## What really costs money: fuel, deposit and extras

The gap between the advertised price and what you actually pay usually comes from three places: fuel, deposit and optional extras. So it does not catch you off guard:

- **License-free boat fuel**: 0 euros, included. This is one of the reasons we recommend this option to most families on their first boat outing.
