## Outreach strategy notes

- Send emails in batches of 5-10 per week, not more (avoid spam flags)
- Personalize at least the first sentence and the "specific article topic" reference
- Track opens/replies; re-engage non-replies after 10 days with a shorter follow-up
- Do NOT send cold outreach to GDPR-protected addresses without a lawful basis (publicly published editorial contact fine)
- Priority queue:
  1. ES travel family blogs (Week 1)
  2. Catalan/Spanish regional media (Week 1)
  3. EN travel blogs Spain-focused (Week 2)
  4. Local hotels/campings (Week 2-3)
  5. Aggregators (Week 3-4)
